Injera

Ethiopia

Ingredients

  • ¾ cup teff flour (found in health food shops)
  • 3½ cups water
  • pinch of salt

Directions

  1. Standing time 2 days
  2. Mix teff with the water. Leave to stand in a covered bowl for a couple of days until it starts to bubble.
  3. The mixture should be slightly sour and the consistency of crêpe batter. Stir in the salt.
  4. Add a ladle of the mixture to a pre-heated skillet and cook slowly until air bubbles rise to the top.
  5. Do not let it brown, and make sure that it is not too thick.
